ABOUT
HELP ONE ANOTHER

Help one another is an organization which is here to help you, be able to help others. When donating money, it sometimes might be hard to find out where to donate, and if the money you donated actually is making an impact. With Help one another you can choose yourself who you want to help out, and actually see how you are positively impacting the life of another person. You will build a more personal connection with the person, and perhaps even make a friend.
